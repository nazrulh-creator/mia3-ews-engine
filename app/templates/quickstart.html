MIA3 Early Warning Engine — Quick Start

Smoke-detector for the guaranteed portfolio · human-on-the-loop · fully auditable

1 · Open it & sign in

  • Hosted: mia3-ews-engine-test.fly.dev (first load wakes the server, ~3–5s).
  • Local: uvicorn app.main:app → 127.0.0.1:8080.
  • Demo logins (TEST, synthetic): internal/internal123, checker/checker123, branch/branch123, fi_mbb/fi123.

5 · The flow

Data arrives → validated against the contract → model scores P(MIA3) → risk score 0.5/0.3/0.2 → four bands → confidence routing → three role-based views.

Risk bands: Low <2.0 Mod 2–3 High 3–3.5 V.High >3.5

2 · Environments — check the colour

  • LIVE CGC-blue bar — real monitoring.
  • TEST CGC-orange bar + watermark + TEST- ids — synthetic sandbox.
  • Separate databases; test data never reaches LIVE.

6 · Models, ensembles & governed settings

  • Models: register synthetic, glass-box logistic/OLS (enter coefficients) or uploaded ML models — one or more active per segment.
  • Decision rules: when several models are active, a governed rule (average, weighted, max, majority…) combines them into the trigger.
  • 50/30/20 weights, band cut-offs, calibration, models and rules are all dual-controlled (a second approver); the dashboard shows which are live.

3 · Your first look (30 seconds)

  1. Sign in as internal.
  2. Demo → Generate & score demo portfolio.
  3. Open the Dashboard — read the band cards and the by-FI / by-sector tables.
  4. Click a total → open an account → read its explanation and arithmetic.

7 · Safety rails

  • Precision is very low (~0.1) by design — high recall means every high-risk flag goes to a human, never an automated action.
  • Borderline-confidence cases are held for review.
  • Every step is written to an append-only, hash-chained audit trail.

4 · What MIA3 does

It watches loans already on the book and scores monthly which are likely to slip into Months-in-Arrears 3, then ranks them by potential impact so attention goes where it matters most. (MicroFlex decides new loans; MIA3 monitors existing ones.)
